Past Projects

Nagarth Charitable Trust — Watershed Management

Worked in ~54 villages of tribal Mhow block. Set up 17 watershed committees (119 members, ~45% women). Activities: PRA, baseline surveys, exposure visits, micro watershed formation and advanced agriculture training.

Indore Municipal Corporation Collaboration

Organized cleaning of stepwells (G.T.S., Kamla Nehru, Champa Bawdi) with ~150 university students. Conducted shramdan events with civic officials.

Community Shramdan for Water Conservation

A Shramdan was organized by Chavi Samajik Sanstha at Maya Khedi Pond and Champa Bawdi in the Lalbagh area. The event was graced by the Commissioner of Indore Municipal Corporation, Mr. Shivam Verma (IAS), Additional Commissioner Mr. Abhilash Mishra (IAS), Chief Engineer Mr. Mahesh Sharma, Health Officer Mr. Virendra Upadhyay, along with local residents and the zonal team

Awareness programs and technical guidance for the installation of rainwater harvesting system

To promote water conservation, Chavi Samajik Sansthan, in collaboration with Indore Municipal Corporation, organized awareness programs and provided technical guidance for the installation of rainwater harvesting systems to improve the city’s groundwater levels. Awareness, rainwater harvesting system installation, and technical guidance were provided across all 22 zones
